CFPB Consumer Complaints

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) is the agency responsible for regulating consumer finance products. They gather complaints from the public and publish the data online. This visualizes the data using the dc-js library. Please note that the whole dataset is loaded into your browser so it takes a little time to download and get running. Once loaded then you can slice and dice to your hearts content.
